Who We Are

Regomoditswe Tshepang Sefako, a 22 year young lady is the beautiful brains behind this beautiful brand called S.H.E Organic Body & Skincare.

S.H.E Organic Body & Skin Care Products are designed by a woman for women but don’t loose your breath yet, men are catered for as well. S.H.E Organic Body & SkinCare is an organic luxury brand designed to help one relax at the same time help boost their skin health. As a lover of Beauty and Skincare, I thought it was fitting to create products that will make people feel free and beautiful in their own skin with no doubt whatsoever and S.H.E Organic Body & Skincare Products promise to deliver exactly that. Please note that none of the products are designed to heal any diagnosed skin condition but to help ease and reduce the appearance of some.
